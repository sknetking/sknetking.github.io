About Raj Auto Garage

Your trusted partner for quality bike repair and maintenance services

Who We Are

Raj Auto Garage is a professional bike repair specialist dedicated to providing exceptional repair and maintenance services for all two-wheeler brands. With years of experience in motorcycle repair, we have built a reputation for quality work, transparent pricing, and customer satisfaction.

Our team of certified technicians specializes in servicing both fuel-injected and air-cooled bikes from all major brands. We believe in combining traditional mechanical expertise with modern diagnostic technology to deliver the best results.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, high-quality bike repair and maintenance services at transparent, competitive prices while prioritizing customer convenience and satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and preferred bike repair service center in the region, known for excellence, integrity, and customer-centric service delivery.
