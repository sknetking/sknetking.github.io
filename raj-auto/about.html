About Raj Auto Garage

Your trusted partner for quality bike and auto repair services

Who We Are

Raj Auto Garage is a professional, multi-brand service center dedicated to providing exceptional repair and maintenance services for bikes and automobiles. With years of experience in the automotive industry, we have built a reputation for quality work, transparent pricing, and customer satisfaction.

Our team of certified technicians specializes in servicing both petrol and electric vehicles from all major brands. We believe in combining traditional mechanical expertise with modern diagnostic technology to deliver the best results.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, high-quality automotive repair and maintenance services at transparent, competitive prices while prioritizing customer convenience and satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and preferred automotive service center in the region, known for excellence, integrity, and customer-centric service delivery.
